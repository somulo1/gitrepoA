VaultKe Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 25, 2025

1. Information We Collect

VaultKe collects information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, join a chama, or use our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services, including chama management, financial tracking, and backup services.

3. Google Drive Integration

When you connect Google Drive, we only access the files we create for your backups. We do not access any other files in your Google Drive.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except as described in this policy.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@vaultke.com

This is a development version. Full privacy policy will be available when the app is publicly released.
